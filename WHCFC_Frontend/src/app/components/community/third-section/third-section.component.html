WHCFC believes in the transformative power of sports, and our vision and focus are to increase football participation while eliminating barriers to this, to give Canadians access to and stay involved in football in communities; this aligns to the Canadian Government initiatives to pursue healthy lifestyles, because it believes that sport plays an important role helping Canadians build the skills they need to grow and thrive physically, mentally and socially. Community members that align to these values will help WHCFC meet its goals and promote FIFA World Cup coming to Toronto in 2026, thereby spreading the passion and fever of football through surrounding communities.
