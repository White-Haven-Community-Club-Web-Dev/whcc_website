WHCC Corp. is more than a football club; it wishes to build and develop and sustain community spirit and synergy by providing free opportunities to its members to not just play football but also participate in tournament festivals as well as free educational workshops organized by WHCC Corp. During tournament festivals, for instance, WHCC Corp. will enable community members to cheer on football teams, enjoy picnics in the park and have fun while getting to know other community members and families, developing relationships with them as well as with local businesses and local politicians. WHCC Corp. will also plan, design and deliver educational sessions/workshops to communities, at no cost, to help them develop critical skills required to succeed in their personal and professional lives, e.g., project management as life skills, mental unity in community, basic computer programming, etc. Community members attending these free sessions will in turn enable their communities to develop as well.
