WHCFC wishes to establish a more accessible and locally organized football program aimed at reducing barriers to participation, particularly among Black and racialized communities, as well as people with low incomes and newcomers. Communities, local businesses and political offices are asked to support this initiative by participating in football opportunities, tournament festivals and educational sessions. They can also show support by helping WHCC Corp. meet its vision and goals via word of mouth promotion of this program and thereby help reduce above-mentioned barriers.
