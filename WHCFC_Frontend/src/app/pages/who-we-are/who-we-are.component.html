About Us

White Haven Community Football Club (WHCFC) is a non-profit organization based in Scarborough, dedicated to providing fun, safe, and inclusive soccer opportunities for all ages and skill levels.

Founded in 2018, we have grown from a small group of soccer enthusiasts to a vibrant community of over 500 active members. Our club offers a place for social interaction, competition, and a shared love of soccer.

Through our various programs and activities, we also strive to promote healthy active lifestyles and fun for all.

Mission

Our mission is simple but impactful: to organize and run recreational & fun-focused soccer programs for our community. By encouraging participation, we promote healthy and active lifestyles.

We believe that everyone should have access to quality sports programs, regardless of their background or circumstances. We achieve this through educational workshops on essential skills like project management, leadership, and teamwork.

Most importantly, we create a welcoming space where people can have fun, make friends, and grow together through the beautiful game of soccer.

Vision

We envision a future where WHCFC becomes a cornerstone of the Scarborough community — not just for football, but for social connection and civic leadership.

Our aim is to develop ambitious club members who will take on important community-building roles. Through leadership development and teamwork, we are promoting youth connection and skill-building.

We work to create spaces for people of all ages to come together, stay active, and build meaningful relationships. We are all on this field together; our progress, we will achieve together, advancing both personally and professionally through the power of sport and community.

Goal

Our primary goal is to create an inclusive organization with affordable access to opportunities for everyone, regardless of their background or circumstances.

We are committed to offering programs that support community building and skill development. Through organized soccer events, workshops, and training sessions, we aim to build stronger, more connected communities.

We also seek to build partnerships with other organizations and stakeholders to expand our reach and enhance our professional development. We aim to build life-long teams and ensure communities in soccer and beyond feel connected and building stronger.
