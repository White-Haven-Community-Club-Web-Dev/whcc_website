Canada Day Youth Soccer Tournament Festival 2025

Join us on Canada day, July 1st 2025, for a full-day soccer festival at White Haven Park. We're building a community through the transformative power of football. Players, their friends and families, members of surrounding neighbourhoods, small business vendors and political leaders will come together for a day of fun, connect with each other and celebrate diversity, nationality, summer, football and community.

REGISTER YOUR TEAM

Join The tournament

On Tuesday July 1, 2025 between 4 and 8 Youth teams will compete for prizes while community members watch them play, picnic in the park and meet their neighbours, local vendors and politicians. Free football clinics for youth and children will be held in between games, and other fun and educational activities will be offered.

Want To Join?

Why WHCC Corp. is Hosting & Organizing This Tournament?

To provide Youth with fun, encourage competition, build skills and empower confidence, and to allow friends, family and Community Members to have:

Community Engagement

Celebrate Canada Day with Scarborough families, businesses, and leaders through food, fun, and football.

Vendor Opportunities

Local vendors can showcase their products and connect with the community in a festive, high-traffic setting.

Meet Your Leaders

Political leaders will drop by to meet residents, listen to ideas, and support local youth and community efforts.

THE ONGOING PROGRAM

Since 2022, the F4 program has offered free drop-in soccer twice a week at White Haven Park. This open scrimmage format welcomes all skill levels to enjoy the game and stay active. Members of F4 and White Haven Football Club will be at the event to share info and welcome new players.
