Educational Sessions


Welcome to WHCC Corp.'s Educational Sessions Program, a cornerstone of our vision to foster community spirit and synergy. As the first club in Scarborough to offer free football and educational opportunities, we are committed to providing a space where residents can connect, grow, learn, and develop through the transformative power of education.

Program Highlights:

  • Project Management as Life Skills
  • Mental Health Awareness
  • Basic Computer Programming
  • Basic Financial Management

These sessions are designed to equip participants with the skills needed to compete for employment and manage their personal and professional lives successfully. By empowering individuals with these competencies, we aim to contribute to the overall development of our community. Join us in this journey of learning and growth, and be part of a community that cares and thrives together.
