Educational Sessions

Welcome to WHCC Corp.'s Educational Sessions Program, a cornerstone of our vision to foster community spirit and synergy. As the first club in Scarborough to offer free football and educational opportunities, we are committed to providing a space where residents can connect, grow, learn, and develop through the transformative power of education.
